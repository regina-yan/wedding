Travel, Stay & Play

Getting Here

Camp Glen Brook is located at 35 Glenbrook Rd, Marlborough, NH 03455.

Like many places in the Upper Valley, it's not close to a major airport or train station (the nearest being Battleboro Amtrak station, about 35 minute drive away). This means the easiest form of transportation is driving. It will be worth it, though, we promise! Our recommendations for getting here:

New York or closer: drive

We recommend driving - the drive is under 4 hours from Manhattan. Half our guests live in New York so if you're looking for carpooling options, reach out to Regina and Sheppard.

New York to DC: drive/fly

For our guests living outside the 4-hour drive radius (Arlington + DC friends especially), the drive is much longer but doable if that's the way you want to go. From DC, it's about an 8 hour drive. Amtrak's Vermonter route runs one 9-hour train between Union Station and Battleboro each day. Battleboro station is about a 45 min drive from camp. If going this route, you could try 8:10am-4:56pm on Friday, and 1:02pm-10:28pm on Sunday. If this were us, we'd probably choose the flight option below.

Further than New York: fly

We'd recommend flying into Boston Logan Airport and renting a car/carpooling. The campm is just under a 2 hour's drive away.

There are plenty of parking spaces at the camp

Lodging

All wedding activities will be hosted at Camp Glen Brook -- Sheppard grew up going to summer camp here, Regina and Sheppard have both volunteered as counselors during the winter camp. It's truly a magical, whimsical place, and we hope you'll join us in staying at this beautiful camp throughout the weekend!. If you don't want to stay at camp, there are hotel/motel options available in Keene.

Camp Glen Brook

Loding is free, but this is a kid's summer camp and rooms will have 4-10 beds, some of them bunked. For more information about staying at camp, head over to the FAQ

Hotel/Motel in Keene

Keene is a cute town a 15 minute drive away. Some lodging options with prices for 2-night stay:

  • Best Western Plus (~$250)
  • Hampton Inn & Suites (~280)
  • Carriage Barn Bed & Breakfast (~$350)

Things to Do

Camp Glen Brook

Channel your inner kid-at-sleepaway-camp and enjoy the various activities on site:

  • swimming in the lake
  • basketball + playground games at the outdoors rec hall
  • books and board games in the Main house living room
  • hiking trails around the camp
  • visiting the maple sugar shack (not operating during this season)

Mt. Monadnock

A 15-20 minute drive from camp, Mount Monadnock offers beautiful views, but you'll have to work for it - the trails are steep! Bring hiking footwear if you plan to go, and don't hike if it's raining.

We recommend: Dublin Trail

  • 4.4 miles out and back
  • 15min drive from camp to trailhead
  • 3 hour hiking estimate
