Schedule

All events will take place at Camp Glen Brook in Marlborough, New Hampshire

This is the current schedule assuming good weather on Saturday. If there are thunderstorms, we might adjust the ceremony by an hour or two to be during a pocket of sunshine. We'll confirm the exact ceremony time by Monday, September 1, but it will be on Saturday.

September 5

Friday

Welcome Campers!

12 PM: Check-in

Welcome to Camp! You can check-in anytime in the main building from noon onwards. You'll get a room assignment + camp map, and you're free to get settled in an enjoy everything Glen Brook has to offer

6:30 PM: Welcome Party

Attire: Garden party.

If Regina & Shep were guests, she would wear a casual floral dress or jeans + nice top. Shep would wear an unstained shirt and birks. Please don't feel pressure to wear anything specific, we literally don't care, we just want to see you!!

Join us for welcome drinks and a grill-out, with lawn games (Dartmouth pong, perhaps?) and a bonfire if weather permits. Note: This means dinner is provided! Will probably be grilling until ~8:30/9

September 6

Saturday

Wedding Day

8-10 AM: Breakfast

Breakfast cooked by Sheppard+Regina and friends!

All Day: Explore

Fun indoor and outdoor activities around camp. We'll give you a list of ideas when you check in.

3:30 PM: Ceremony

Attire: Cocktail

If Regina & Shep were guests, she would wear a floral long cotton dress, or a satin midi dress if she's feeling bold (BYO steamers/irons - camp doesn't have any). Shep would wear a button-down and long pants hopefully. For those wanting to wear heels, we recommend not wearing stilletos! Think chunky heels or platforms.

The ceremony will take place at 3:30pm in the meadow by the lake (rain option: barn). There is a slight hill to walk down towards the lake.

4 PM: Cocktail Hour-and-a-half

Join us after the ceremony just down the hill for some drinks and light bites before dinner and dancing

5:30-10 PM: Reception

Yummy dinner cooked by the Koufis family, served outdoors (weather permitting) followed by dancing in the barn.

11 PM: After Party?!

September 7

Sunday

So Long!

9-11 AM: Breakfast

Sheppard+Regina and friends will cook breakfast again!

12 PM: Check-Out

Please have cabins cleared out by noon.
