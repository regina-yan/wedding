Schedule

All events will take place at Camp Glen Brook in Marlborough, New Hampshire

This is a tentative schedule - we'll have the exact timing nailed down soon, but expect the activities and approximate time of day to remain the same.

September 5

Friday

Welcome Campers!

12 PM: Check-in

Welcome to Camp! You can check-in anytime in the main building from noon onwards. You'll get a room assignment + camp map, and you're free to get settled in an enjoy everything Glen Brook has to offer

6:30 PM: Welcome Party

Attire: Casual, wear whatever you want

Join us for welcome drinks and a grill-out, with lawn games (Dartmouth pong, perhaps?) and a bonfire if weather permits

September 6

Saturday

Wedding Day

8-10 AM: Breakfast

Breakfast cooked by Sheppard+Regina and friends!

All Day: Explore

Fun indoor and outdoor activities around camp. We'll give you a list of ideas when you check in.

3:30 PM: Ceremony

Attire: Anything from cocktail to formal, no white please!

The ceremony will take place at 3:30pm in the meadow by the lake. There is a slight hill to walk down towards the lake. For those wanting to wear heels, we recommend not wearing stilletos! Think chunky heels or platforms.

4 PM: Cocktail Hour-and-a-half

Join us after the ceremony just down the hill for some drinks and light bites before dinner and dancing

5:30-10 PM: Reception

Yummy dinner cooked by the Koufis family, served outdoors (weather permitting) followed by dancing in the barn.

11 PM: After Party?!

September 7

Sunday

So Long!

9-11 AM: Breakfast

Sheppard+Regina and friends will cook breakfast again!

12 PM: Check-Out

Please have cabins cleared out by noon.
