Our Story

Shep popping the question!

Regina and Sheppard met in 2015 when they were assigned the same freshman year dorm building at Dartmouth. Fate, don't you think? They got to know each other through study sessions, climbing gym hangouts, and eventually their first date at a Bernie Sanders rally where Regina pulled the ol' "yawn and stretch your arm around his shoulder" trick... and it worked!

They started dating in 2016 and the rest is history. They've lived in Washington DC, New York, San Francisco, San Diego, Kansas, Hawaii, and Paris, and shared a love for the outdoors with surfing, climbing, and biking.

In December 2024, Shep completely surprised Regina with a trip to Hawaii where he proposed to her on the beach. Regina cried a lot. Then they biked up Haleakala, a 10,000 foot sea-to-summit climb (during which Shep may have cried a lot) which seemed a fitting way to celebrate their love story.

Enjoy these photos from their 9 years of love, laughter, adventure, and tickles .. and here's to many more (well, maybe not the tickles)!
